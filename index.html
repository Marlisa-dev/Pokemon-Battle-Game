How To Play

1. Click on the Choose Pokemon Button to start the game
2. You will get a random Pokemon loaded onto your card with a picture, name and stats
3. Next, click on the Choose Enemy Button
4. Your Enemy will get a random bokemon loaded onto the enemy card with a picture, name and stats
5. Whoever gets the Pokemon with the higher stats wins the round
6. After 3 rounds, whoever wins the most rounds in the game
7. If your Pokemoen and the enemy pokemon for the round has the same stats, the round becomes a draw and you get an extra round to play
8. To start a new gamw, click on the Load New Game button
